02 About

Curiosity is the
most powerful
compiler.

Like many, I had countless ambitions growing up — cardiologist, Air Force pilot, even a full-time gaming streamer. Everything shifted when I discovered you could modify a phone's OS to improve gaming performance.

That single act of curiosity led me down a rabbit hole of understanding how machines think — and how to speak their language. Eventually that language became my craft.

When I build something, I pause and ask: will this truly help someone? If yes, it deserves to be the best version possible. No compromises. That's the standard I hold every line of code to.
